Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you have a physical store I can visit?

No, CloudBake operates as a cloud kitchen which means we don't have a physical storefront. This allows us to focus all our resources on creating the best possible pastries and delivering them fresh to your door!

How far in advance should I order custom cakes?

We recommend ordering custom cakes at least 72 hours in advance. For complex designs or large orders, please give us 5-7 days notice to ensure we can create your perfect cake.

What are your delivery hours and fees?

We deliver daily from 10am to 8pm. Delivery is free for orders over $40. For orders under $40, there's a $4.99 delivery fee. You can track your order in real-time once it's out for delivery.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes! We have a variety of gluten-free and vegan options. When ordering through our Custom Cake Maker or in the special instructions, just let us know about your dietary requirements and we'll accommodate them.
